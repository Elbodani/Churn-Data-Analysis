## Project Overview
The primary goal of this analysis is to decode the 27% Churn Rate within a customer base of 6,418. By visualizing churn through various lenses—such as 
contract types, service categories, and specific customer pain points.The project identifies exactly where the business is losing revenue and why.

Key Insights & Findings
### 1. High-Risk Segments
Contract Type: Customers on Month-to-Month contracts have a staggering 46.5% churn rate, compared to just 2.7% for those on two-year plans.

Payment Method: Mailed checks (37.8%) and Bank Withdrawals (34.4%) see significantly higher churn than credit card users.

Demographics: Older customers (Age 50+) represent the highest churn demographic at 31%.

### 2. The "Competitor" Factor
The "Churn Distribution" section reveals that Competitors are the #1 reason for customer loss (761 total churns). Drill-down data shows:

Better Devices: 289 customers left for better hardware.

Better Offers: 274 customers were swayed by superior competitor pricing or bundles.

### 3. Service Impact
Internet Type: Fiber Optic users churn at a rate of 41.1%, suggesting possible issues with service stability or pricing in that specific tech tier.

Technical Support: Churn is notably lower among customers who utilize Premium Support and Online Security services, highlighting these as key retention tools.

## Dashboard Features
Executive Summary: High-level KPIs showing Total Customers, New Joiners, Total Churn, and Churn Rate.

Churn Reason Drill-down: Interactive tooltips that break down broad categories (like "Dissatisfaction") into specific causes (like "Network Reliability").

Geographic Analysis: A Top 5 leaderboard showing churn by state, identifying Jammu as a critical area (57.2% churn).

Service Usage Matrix: A detailed heat map comparing various service add-ons and their correlation with churn.

## Technologies Used
Power BI: Data visualization, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom measures, and interactive reporting.

Data Modeling: Cleaning and structuring raw customer datasets to establish relationships between demographics and service usage.

## Recommendations based on Data
1. Promote Long-term Contracts: Offer incentives to migrate Month-to-Month users to 1-year or 2-year plans.

2. Competitor Matching: Review device offerings and promotional pricing to counter the heavy loss of customers to competitors.

3. Targeted Support: Focus on the "Fiber Optic" segment to identify if the high churn is due to technical outages or price sensitivity.
